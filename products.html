Digital marketing

Digital marketing is the promotion and selling of products or services through various digital channels such as websites, social media, search engines, mobile apps, and email. It uses online platforms to connect brands with potential customers, leveraging data-driven strategies to deliver personalized and relevant messages. Unlike traditional marketing, digital marketing allows businesses to target specific audiences, measure campaign effectiveness in real time, and adapt quickly to market changes. It includes techniques like search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, social media advertising, email campaigns, and pay-per-click (PPC) advertising.
